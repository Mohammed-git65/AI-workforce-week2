**2. Assigning a License**
   
  •	Select the created user in Active users.

  <img width="916" height="585" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/584324c2-c940-45b5-beb0-57014cee9bf7" />


  •	Click Licenses and apps.

  <img width="888" height="560" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/15949923-f10f-4cd3-b36b-e284fe74370c" />


  •	Choose an available Microsoft 365 license.

  •	Enable or disable specific services (e.g., Exchange, Teams).

  •	Save changes to assign the license.

**4. Benefits of Assigning a License**

  •	Activates access to Microsoft 365 apps and services.

  •	Enables email, OneDrive storage, Teams, and other features.

  •	Ensures users can collaborate and work productively.

  •	Helps organizations manage access, security, and costs efficiently
